Research

Kriyatmak(physiological) effects of Ratraujagran in night shift workers with respect to Inflammatory biomarkers C-Reactive Protein and Erythrocyte Sedimentation Rate-An observational study.

01. Role of Ratraujagran in physiological changes

Ratraujagran is a major causative factor of many diseases however ‘How it affects physiology first?’ is not elaborated, slow and long-term exposure of ratraujagran affects physiology and then the pathology starts to develop in turn manifesting in a major disease

02. Knowledge Gap

Healthy Individual not having any sleep complaints i.e., having samanya nidra vega but has to remain awake due to night shift duties i.e., nidra vega dharan is done by an individual. In such healthy individuals whose dinacharya,ratraucharya has been disturbed the physiological effects of ratraujagran i.e ,nidra vega dharan lakshanas will be observed.

03. Primary objective

To review literature for detail understanding of effects of Ratraujagran with respect to kriya sharir attributes and also to observe physiological effects in individuals working in night shift.
