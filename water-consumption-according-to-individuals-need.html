Water consumption according to individuals need.

  Dr. Akshay Lonkar

You must have got some advice from your friends, and relatives regarding the consumption of water i.e., you must drink a large quantity of water but have you thought that how much water one must consume, the answer to the above query is the same as how much quantity of food one must consume.

One must consume water only according to his body requirements, so you must be thinking about how to identify the daily demand for water requirement, so here is the answer the demand for water depends on your age, Prakriti, Sahara(food habits), occupation etc.,

How the demand of the body for one working in the sun or the field and the other working in an office in AC how would the demand for water for both be the same?

Yes the demand for both will be different and you must be thinking about how to decide when to drink water, one should drink water only when he is thirsty and avoid excess drinking of water, and one should sit on a chair and drink water slowly by touching it to the palate.

There are some misconceptions in society that drinking a large quantity of water detoxifies the body, but do you think that the human body acts as a flush tank is it so? No, the human body is not a flush and all that is consumed through the mouth needs to be metabolised.

There has been a trend set in the society and that is of drinking water early in the morning on an empty stomach or drinking a large quantity of water before going to bed. But have you ever imagined or thought about what must be happening when one behaves such? Just Imagine a fire which is burning and one puts water on it what would happen?

After dinner, an individual should not consume anything till the next morning not even the water without any requirement. Drinking water at night gives rise to various diseases of Ear, Nose, Throat and eyes. Even it would give rise to cold and swelling of the face.

Early in the morning when we are empty stomachs there is a large gap between night meal and drinking water, early morning acts the same as we do with the fire i.e, we fulfil our hunger just by drinking water and diluting the gastric secretions required for digestion of food.

During the Rainy season and winter, we don't feel much thirsty but it doesn't mean that our body doesn't have sensations or knowledge of when to drink water, Is our body that foolish?

Finally, the conclusion is to try to avoid excess water consumption and drink it only when the body demands i.e when we feel thirsty.

Try following this and it will help you to maintain your health and also to reduce overconsumption of water.
